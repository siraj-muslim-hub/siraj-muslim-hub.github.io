Questions

Good to know

Is Sirāj free?

Yes — every worship feature is free, with no ads. An optional Supporter subscription helps fund development and unlocks cloud sync across your devices; it adds no walls to the core features.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Prayer times, the Qur'an, Ḥadīth, Duʿāʾ, the 99 Names and the Hijri calendar all work without a connection. Your data stays on your device unless you sign in for sync.

Where do the prayer times come from?

They're computed locally from your location using established methods (Muslim World League, Umm al-Qurā, ISNA, Egyptian and more), with Ḥanafī/Shāfiʿī Asr options and manual adjustments.

Is the religious content authentic?

Yes. The Qur'an uses the Uthmānī script from Tanzil with established translations, and the Ḥadīth are taken verbatim from verified collections. Nothing sacred is machine-generated.

Which languages are supported?

English and Arabic are fully maintained, with translations for Urdu, Turkish, French, Indonesian, Malay and Hausa — and full right-to-left layout for Arabic and Urdu.
